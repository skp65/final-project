Google LLC is multinational technology company that specializes in internet-related service and products such as: search engine, cloud computing, software and hardware. It is considered one of the Big 5 technology companies. It was founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin. Google became famous for its search engine and is also the world's most visited website. It's because of the following things:


  • It has better search experience.
  • Great Business Exposure.
  • It has innovative Products and Services.
