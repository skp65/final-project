There are many websites that have been available in the internet to almost everybody since the past 20 years. The popularity of each website is different and is determined by number of users visited that website, etc.
The top three websites worldwide that has taken over in terms of visits are:

Google, YouTube & Facebook

Google

Google is a company who is mostly famous for creating the search engine called google.com. It was created in 1998 and 22 years in the future it is the number one website in the world that everybody visits to google/search things of their interest.

YouTube

Youtube was initially started by three PayPal employees above a pizzeria and a Japanese restaurant in California in May 2005. After 15 years this website was the number one streaming website for videos and number 2 as the most visited website due to its nature of streaming videos.

Facebook

Facebook was launched in 2004 which is a online social media founded by Mark Zuckerberg along with his four roommates. It initially started its access for Harvard college students then slowly it expanded to other universities and high school. Since then it has come a long way that it became number third website around the world.
